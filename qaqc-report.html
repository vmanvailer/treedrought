Climate data preparation

First steps involve preparing climate data to derive a moisture index. (Step 1) Shift calendar year months to better align with growth period. Northern hemisphere 1971 year starts in September 1970 and ends in August of 1971 Southern hemisphere 1971 year starts in March 1971 and ends in Februrary 1972 (Step 2) Calculate monthly: a. Potential Evapotranpiration (PET), b. Water Balance (BAL), and c. Standardized Preciptation-Evapotranspiration Index (SPEI) (Step 3) Aggregate monthly climate to yearly to match tree ring temporal resolution.

Expand dataset

Dataset is expanded to make sure every drought year of every site can have growth data aggregated into average pre-drought growth (2 years) and average pos-drought growth (2 years)

Calculate RRR

Resistance, Recovery and Resilience indices are calculated based on drought years for each site.

Model RRR

Resilience is modeled using negative exponential function.

Calculate RED50

Resilience at 50% resistance (RED50) is predicted for each site

Summarise RED50

RED50 is aggregated to group level.
